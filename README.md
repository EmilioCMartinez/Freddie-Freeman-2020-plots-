# Freddie-Freeman-2020-plots-
Freddie Freeman Plots for 2020 batting metrics 



Freddie Freeman had 49% of his batted ball events between 8 and 32 degrees and 54% of his batted ball events at 95mph EV or greater. These are both absurd percentages. (This turns out to be an avg EV of 92.4 mph and avg LA of 17.2 degrees)

The scatter plot highlights his batted ball events: The black vertical line marks 95mph EV and the yellow rectangle highlights 8-32 degrees LA. The red circles show balls that are classified as balls that are hard hit and labeled as sweet spot as well. This is all batted ball events >=95mph EV and between 8-32degrees LA
